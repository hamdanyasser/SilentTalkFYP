## Welcome to SilentTalk

SilentTalk is a comprehensive communication platform designed to bridge the gap between deaf/hard-of-hearing individuals and the hearing community. Our platform provides:

- **Real-time sign language recognition** powered by AI
- **Multi-party video calls** with live captions
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** for spoken communication
- **Educational resources** for learning sign language
- **Community forum** for connecting with others

### Who is SilentTalk for?

- **Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals** seeking accessible communication
- **Sign language users** wanting to communicate with non-signers
- **Hearing individuals** learning sign language
- **Interpreters** providing professional services
- **Educators** teaching sign language
- **Healthcare providers** serving deaf patients
- **Businesses** committed to accessibility

## Sign Language Recognition

### How It Works

SilentTalk uses AI-powered computer vision to recognize sign language gestures in real-time:

1. **Camera captures** your hand movements
2. **AI analyzes** hand shapes and motions
3. **Text appears** as live captions
4. **Speech synthesis** converts to audio (optional)

### Using Sign Recognition

**Enable Sign Recognition:**
1. Join or start a video call
2. Click **"Captions"** button (üí¨)
3. Sign recognition automatically starts
4. Begin signing naturally

**Best Practices for Accurate Recognition:**

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Sign within camera frame
- Use good lighting (front-lit, not backlit)
- Maintain clear background
- Sign at moderate speed
- Face the camera directly
- Keep hands visible

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Sign too fast or too slow
- Block hands with objects
- Use busy/cluttered background
- Have poor lighting
- Sign partially off-screen

**Supported Sign Languages:**
- American Sign Language (ASL)
- British Sign Language (BSL)
- International Sign (IS)
- *(More languages coming soon)*

**Recognition Accuracy:**

Average accuracy: **87%**

Factors affecting accuracy:
- Lighting conditions
- Camera quality
- Internet speed
- Sign clarity
- Background complexity

### Troubleshooting Recognition Issues

**Problem: No captions appearing**
- Check camera permissions
- Ensure captions enabled
- Check internet connection
- Try adjusting camera angle

**Problem: Inaccurate recognition**
- Improve lighting
- Clear background
- Sign more deliberately
- Check camera is 720p or better

**Problem: Captions lagging**
- Check internet speed (minimum 5 Mbps)
- Close other applications
- Move closer to WiFi router
- Reduce video quality if needed

## Troubleshooting

### Video Call Issues

**Problem: Can't join video call**
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check camera/microphone permissions
- Restart browser

**Problem: No video showing**
- Check camera permissions
- Ensure camera not used by another app
- Try Settings ‚Üí Audio/Video ‚Üí Test Camera
- Update browser to latest version

**Problem: Audio not working**
- Check microphone permissions
- Unmute microphone in call
- Test microphone in settings
- Check system sound settings
- Try different headset/microphone

**Problem: Poor video quality**
- Check internet speed (minimum 5 Mbps)
- Move closer to WiFi router
- Close other applications using bandwidth
- Lower video quality: Settings ‚Üí Video Quality ‚Üí Low
- Use wired connection if possible

### Sign Recognition Issues

**Problem: Signs not recognized**
- Ensure good lighting
- Clear background
- Keep hands in frame
- Sign more slowly
- Check internet connection

**Problem: Captions delayed**
- Check internet speed
- Reduce video quality
- Close background apps
- Move closer to router

### Account Issues

**Problem: Can't log in**
- Check username/email spelling
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cookies
- Check email for verification link
- Contact support if persists

**Problem: Forgot password**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click link and set new password

**Problem: Email not verified**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Resend Verification Email

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is SilentTalk free?**
A: Yes, SilentTalk offers a free tier with core features. Premium features available with paid subscription.

**Q: What sign languages are supported?**
A: Currently ASL, BSL, and International Sign. More languages being added.

**Q: Can I use SilentTalk on mobile?**
A: Yes, web app works on mobile browsers. Native mobile apps coming soon.

**Q: Do I need to download anything?**
A: No, SilentTalk runs in your web browser. No downloads required.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use end-to-end encryption, secure servers, and follow GDPR compliance.

### Video Calling

**Q: How many people can join a video call?**
A: Up to 10 participants on free plan, 50 on premium plan.

**Q: Can I record calls?**
A: Yes, with participant consent. All participants notified when recording starts.

**Q: Do I need high-speed internet?**
A: Minimum 5 Mbps recommended. Lower speeds may reduce video quality.

### Sign Recognition

**Q: How accurate is sign recognition?**
A: Average 87% accuracy. Accuracy improves with good lighting and clear signing.

**Q: Can it recognize fingerspelling?**
A: Yes, but accuracy is lower for rapid fingerspelling.

**Q: Does it work in low light?**
A: Recognition accuracy decreases in low light. Good lighting essential.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
A: Depends on privacy settings. Default is "Friends Only".

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes, Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Delete Account. 30-day grace period.

**Q: Is video call encrypted?**
A: Yes, all video calls use WebRTC with encryption.
